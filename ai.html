Favourite fruit

Mango: the king of fruits

Sweet, juicy, and ridiculously satisfying—mango is my undisputed number one. Every season feels better with a perfectly ripe mango.

Why I love mango

  • Flavour: That mix of sweetness and tropical tang is unbeatable.
  • Texture: Smooth, juicy, and soft when it’s perfectly ripe.
  • Versatility: Amazing in smoothies, desserts, salads—or just eaten over the sink.

More Fun Facts

  • Mangoes are related to cashews and pistachios.
  • There are over 500+ mango varieties worldwide.
  • Mango is the national fruit of India, Pakistan, and the Philippines.
  • The oldest mango tree is over 300 years old and still produces fruit.
  • Mangoes were first grown in India over 4,000 years ago.
🥭 Fun fact: There are hundreds of mango varieties—each with its own flavour, aroma, and personality.
